      About

      Decapsulate is a discussion podcast between Tris Oaten and Robin Winslow.

      Meet The Hosts

      Tris

      Tris is a producer, web developer, and musician based in London. He writes electronic music, fast technical videos, produces an urban fantasy podcast called Modem Prometheus, and a hopepunk podcast called Lost Terminal, as well as a magical realism art history podcast, The Phosphene Catalogue.

      He maintains a digital garden at - namtao.com, and can be contacted on Mastodon @tris@namtao.com

      Robin

      After spending the previous decade at the Ubuntu corporation, Robin now leads a software team at a breakthrough genetics outfit, alongside volunteering for a great local refugee charity.

      Undeterred by precent, Robin still believes he might show his ailing blog some love someday soon. In the meantime, he’s delighted to use chatting to Tris as the latest excuse for his writer’s block.

      Robin also theoretically has a Mastodon account, although if you really want to get in touch with him, email might be your best bet. (He very rarely checks his LinkedIn account).
